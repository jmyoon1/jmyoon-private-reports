Dominant approach found

The respect/replace split is structural. The merit-proportional FairX line (Wang & Joachims and successors) REPLACES the scorer — merit is a function of the bandit's own estimated means — and under trusted immutable scores its learning problem degenerates to a closed-form target plus SP4 realization. The genuine score-free column is the quota-floor family: Fair-Learn gives the strongest realized-exposure guarantee in the entire survey (deterministic, anytime, learner-independent count floor at O(1)/round); EARS and the YouTube Shorts satisfaction filter are score-read-only layers.

Surprise / Confirmation

(a) Exactly one position-weighted regret bound exists in the whole bandit line (ConcaveRewards, objective-side only) — the count→position gap survives its 4th SP; SP7 is the final check. (b) Bandit bounds do NOT close SP4's open-loop concentration gap: realized-count guarantees are again closed-loop ledger mechanisms (uuid 18 confirmed in a 4th literature). (c) Non-stationary arrivals + estimated relevance are handled at most one at a time, never with slates. (d) Production cold-start over-exposure confirmed (YouTube Shorts), with an inversion: FEWER, better-matched impressions won — confirming exposure→utility heterogeneity (uuid 8). (e) Exploration noise does not debias exposure (CascadingExposure) — debiasing claims resting on exploration alone are suspect.
